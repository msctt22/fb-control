Calorie menu

kcal per item for point-of-choice display
England requires businesses with 250+ employees to show kcal at the point a guest chooses, plus the line "Adults need around 2000 kcal a day". Alcoholic drinks above 1.2% ABV are exempt from mandatory display, so they're listed separately below for your information. Treat figures as estimates from your recipe data.

Settings

GP% is calculated on the net (ex-VAT) selling price, the standard UK hospitality method: GP% = (net price − cost) ÷ net price. Applies to both food and beverage items.
